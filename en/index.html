Our Story

The land, Antonis and the rhythm of each season

At Antonis' Garden, production is not just a job — it's a daily relationship with the land of Moria. Every crop follows its natural season, with care, persistence and respect for flavor.

Our goal is to give the people of Lesvos fresh, honest and quality products, without exaggerations and without distance from the producer.
